Hilton Worldwide first entered China with the opening of Hilton Shanghai in 1988. Over the past 25 years, we’ve grown to 34 properties across four brands, including Waldorf Astoria Hotels & Resorts, Conrad Hotels & Resorts, Hilton Hotels & Resorts and DoubleTree by Hilton, and are on course to have a presence in 75 cities by 2015.

As we’ve grown, so have our guests. Together, through major milestones and millions of everyday moments, we have shared in so many treasured memories together. On this special anniversary we thank you for sharing the past 25 years with us and look forward to the journey ahead.
