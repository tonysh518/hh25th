YOUR LOYALTY DESERVES TO BE REWARDED
  • Happy 25th Birthday, Hilton Shanghai!
    To kick off 25 Weeks of Rewards, we’re going back to where it all began: Hilton Shanghai. This week, Hilton HHonors will be rewarding 5 lucky members with a two-night stay in a King Deluxe Suite at Hilton Shanghai. Simply send an email to marketing.asiapacific@hilton.com with the subject line “Happy Birthday Hilton Shanghai” and include your HHonors membership number for the chance to win!
  • The Hong Kong Getaway
    If you’ve never seen the sun set over Hong Kong from the top of Victoria Peak, then this is the week for you. HHonors is rewarding one lucky member in China with a trip to Hong Kong, including two roundtrip flight tickets and 250,000 HHonors Points to book a stay at Conrad Hong Kong. Check out the Hilton HHonors Sina Weibo account on July 15 for more information on how to win.
  • The Upgrade
    Complimentary room upgrades, free Wi-Fi and breakfast are just a few of the perks of HHonors membership. This week, we’ll be surprising 25 members at check-in with membership status upgrades. What’s more, we’ll upgrade 25 Diamond members to the best-available suite upon check-in and give them 25,000 HHonors Points each.
  • Executive Lounge Happy Hour
    For one week only, participating hotels across China are opening their Executive Lounges to all members for an evening happy hour. Not a member yet? Enroll today! Check out our Sina Weibo account on July 29 to find out how you could also win 50,000 HHonors Points during this week!
  • The HHonors Multi-brand Experience
    At Hilton HHonors, we have a brand for our guests’ every budget and occasion. Haven’t tried them all yet? Enter our Sina Weibo contest to win a set of hotel vouchers valid for a one-night stay each at a property in China from Waldorf Astoria Hotels & Resorts, Conrad Hotels & Resorts, Hilton Hotels & Resorts and DoubleTree by Hilton.

At Hilton HHonors, we believe that membership is about more than just points. It’s about taking your hard earned points and turning them into experiences worth sharing with your loved ones.

From July 8 through December 29, 2013, Hilton HHonors will surprise and delight members across China with 25 Weeks of Rewards, giving away free nights, HHonors Points, membership upgrades, incredible getaways and so much more. Isn’t it time you experienced the world of Hilton HHonors?
