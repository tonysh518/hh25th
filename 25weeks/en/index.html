    25 Weeks of Rewards
  • Hilton HHonors Shopping Mall Giveaway (September 16-22)
    Did you know that you can redeem your HHonors Points online for cameras, travel accessories, great gifts and more with the Hilton HHonors Shopping Mall? This week, we are giving away 25 items from the Hilton HHonors Shopping Mall to our members in China. Enter our Sina Weibo contest on September 16 for your chance to bring one of the gifts home!
  • The HHonors Multi-brand Experience (September 23-29)
    At Hilton HHonors, we have a brand for our guests’ every budget and occasion. Haven’t tried them all yet? The multi-brand experience giveaway is back! Enter our Sina Weibo contest to win a set of hotel vouchers valid for a one-night stay each at a property in China from Waldorf Astoria Hotels & Resorts, Conrad Hotels & Resorts, Hilton Hotels & Resorts and DoubleTree by Hilton.
  • HHonors Fast Track (September 30-October 6)
    Did you know that Hilton HHonors Gold members receive complimentary in-room internet access, complimentary breakfast, 25% point bonus and space-available room upgrades? This week, 25 Hilton HHonors Blue members will be upgraded to Gold status. Visit our Sina Weibo account on September 30 to find out how you can make this year’s Golden Week even more memorable!
  • Flavours of China Giveaway (October 7-13)
    From the ancient ruins of the Terracotta Warriors in Xi’an to the dazzling lights of Victoria Harbor in Hong Kong to the historic grandeur of the Forbidden City in Beijing, China’s wonders are endless. This week, Hilton HHonors is giving away 10 Flavours of China packages, including one-night accommodation, breakfast for two and tickets to a local attraction. Pick your favorite destination in China and enter our Sina Weibo contest for a chance to win an unforgettable trip!
  • The Free Night (October 14-20)
    Are you just shy of enough HHonors Points to take your dream vacation? This week, we’re making it even easier for you to get away by giving away 50,000 HHonors Points to 25 lucky winners! HHonors Points can be redeemed for any room, any time, anywhere at more than 4,000 hotels and resorts around the world. More details to come on October 14 on Sina Weibo.

At Hilton HHonors, we believe that membership is about more than just points. It’s about taking your hard earned points and turning them into experiences worth sharing with your loved ones.

From July 8 through December 29, 2013, Hilton HHonors will surprise and delight members across China with 25 Weeks of Rewards, giving away free nights, HHonors Points, membership upgrades, incredible getaways and so much more. Isn’t it time you experienced the world of Hilton HHonors?
