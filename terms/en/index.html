To be eligible for this offer, you must (1) be both a member of Hilton HHonors and Velocity Frequent Flyer programs, (2) select Virgin Australia as your Double Dip earnings partner in your HHonors Profile, and (3) be a HHonors member to earn Points & Miles for hotel stays. The Promotion commences at 9:00am AEST on 01/05/2013 and concludes at 11:59pm AEST on 31/07/2013 (“Promotional Period”). Entry is open to residents of Australia and New Zealand aged 18 years or older who are members of both the Velocity Frequent Flyer program (“Velocity Member”) and members of the Hilton HHonors Program (“HHonors member”), collectively “Members”. Employees (and their immediate families) of the Promoter, Hilton Worldwide and agencies associated with this competition are ineligible to enter. To receive an entry, Members must register at HHonors.com/stayandwin prior to check-out of their first Qualifying Stay at a Participating Hotel during the Promotional Period. There will be one (1) winner for each calendar month of the Promotional Period (each a “Monthly Winner”). There will be three (3) Monthly Winners in total for this Promotion. The Monthly Winner will be the Member with the highest number of Velocity Points credited to their Velocity Account for Qualifying Stay(s) at Participating Hotel(s) for that calendar month. A Velocity Member is not eligible to be a Monthly Winner more than once during the Promotional Period. Prize for Month 1 (01/05/2013 – 31/05/2013) is two (2) return economy flights to Surfers Paradise on Virgin Australia (departing from the winner’s closest port) valued at up to AU$ 1,368 and 4 nights’ accommodation and daily breakfast for 2 people at the Hilton Surfers Paradise valued at up to AU$3,256. Prize for Month 2 (01/06/2013 – 30/06/2013) is two (2) return economy flights to Queenstown, New Zealand on Virgin Australia (departing from the winner’s closest port) valued at up to AU$ 3,716 and 4 nights’ accommodation and daily breakfast for 2 people at the Hilton Queenstown valued at up to NZ$956. Prize for Month 3 (01/07/2013 – 31/07/2013) is two (2) return economy flights to Nadi, Fiji on Virgin Australia (departing from the winner’s closest port) valued at up to AU$4,860, and 4 nights’ accommodation and daily breakfast for 2 people at Fiji Beach Resort & Spa – Managed by Hilton valued at up to FJ$2,036. Flights will be awarded in the form of a Virgin Australia prize certificate. Redemption of the Virgin Australia flight prize certificate is subject to any terms and conditions of Virgin Australia including those specified on the Prize Certificate. The three (3) winners will be identified at Velocity Rewards, 56 Edmondstone Road, Bowen Hills, QLD 4006 on 15/08/2013 at 11.00am AEST.
